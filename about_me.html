I am originally from the Alsace region in France (at the German border) and I have been living in the US for 13 years. I live in Dallas, Texas where I teach French and translation. I have been teaching for 23 years and I became interested in coding because of localization. When you translate a website or software into another language, the contents need to be localized and the code needs to be checked for errors or bugs. I think technology will keep on evolving in all aspects of human life in the future and understanding how it works and contributing to it is gratifying.
